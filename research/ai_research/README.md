# AI Applications Future Research Project

## Mission
Research and build a comprehensive understanding of AI application features expected in the future, with the goal of delivering AI power to common people.

## Timeline
**Duration:** 6 weeks (Started: 2026-04-22)  
**Daily Check-in:** 9:00 PM IST (3:30 PM UTC)  
**End Goal:** Business model + Revenue plan for AI-powered application accessible to common people

## Research Structure

### Weekly Focus Areas
- **Week 1:** Current AI landscape, user needs, accessibility patterns
- **Week 2:** Emerging technologies, patent analysis, innovation trends
- **Week 3:** User experience patterns, interaction models, voice/multimodal
- **Week 4:** Business models, monetization strategies, market analysis
- **Week 5:** Technical architecture, scalability, deployment strategies
- **Week 6:** Final synthesis, business plan, revenue model, go-to-market

## Research Sources
- Web research (articles, blogs, case studies)
- Academic papers & research publications
- Patent databases (Google Patents, USPTO, etc.)
- Industry reports & whitepapers
- Competitor analysis
- User surveys & feedback platforms

## Daily Process
1. **Morning:** Review previous day's learnings
2. **Day:** Deep research (3-4 hours focused work)
3. **Evening:** Synthesize findings into MD files
4. **9 PM IST:** Daily check-in with research summary

## Key Questions to Answer
1. What features do users expect from AI applications in 2026-2030?
2. How can AI be made accessible to common people (non-technical)?
3. What business models work for AI applications at scale?
4. What technical architecture supports scalable, affordable AI delivery?
5. How do we monetize while keeping it accessible?
6. What are the competitive advantages we can build?

## Success Criteria
- Comprehensive understanding of AI application landscape
- Clear user persona & needs analysis
- Validated business model
- Revenue projections & pricing strategy
- Technical feasibility assessment
- Go-to-market plan
