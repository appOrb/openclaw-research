## Initial Insights

### What "Common Man" Needs from AI
1. **Simplicity:** No technical setup, works immediately
2. **Natural Interaction:** Voice, text, visual - not code
3. **Practical Value:** Solves real daily problems
4. **Affordability:** Free tier or very low cost
5. **Trust:** Privacy protection, transparent

### Potential Focus Areas
- Voice-first AI applications (accessibility)
- Visual AI (no reading required)
- Context-aware assistance (proactive help)
- Multilingual by default
- Works offline (for areas with poor connectivity)
