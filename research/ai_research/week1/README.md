# Week 1 Research Plan

## Focus: Current AI Landscape & User Needs

### Objectives
1. Map the current AI application ecosystem
2. Identify user personas & their needs
3. Understand accessibility requirements
4. Analyze successful models for common people

### Daily Topics

#### Day 1 (2026-04-22) - Foundation
- Current AI application categories
- User demographics & technical levels
- Initial accessibility research

#### Day 2 (2026-04-23) - User Pain Points
- Common barriers to AI adoption
- User feedback from existing AI apps
- Technical vs non-technical user needs

#### Day 3 (2026-04-24) - Accessibility Deep Dive
- Voice interfaces & natural language
- Visual accessibility (low/no vision users)
- Touch & gesture interfaces
- Multi-modal interaction patterns

#### Day 4 (2026-04-25) - Successful Case Studies
- ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini adoption patterns
- Consumer AI apps (Replika, Character.AI)
- Enterprise AI tools that work for everyone

#### Day 5 (2026-04-26) - Privacy & Trust
- Privacy concerns in AI applications
- Data handling expectations
- Trust-building mechanisms
- Ethical AI considerations

#### Day 6 (2026-04-27) - Cost & Business Models
- Free tier strategies
- Freemium models in AI
- Usage-based pricing
- Subscription models that work

#### Day 7 (2026-04-28) - Week 1 Synthesis
- Consolidate findings
- Create user personas
- Draft initial requirements
- Prepare for Week 2

### Research Sources
- Web: TechCrunch, The Verge, AI news sites
- Academic: arXiv, ACM Digital Library
- Industry: Gartner, McKinsey AI reports
- Patents: Google Patents, USPTO
- User feedback: Reddit, Product Hunt, App Store reviews
